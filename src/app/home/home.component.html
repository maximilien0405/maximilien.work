Hello ! I'm Maximilian. I am currently in apprenticeship in Geneva to obtain my CFC as a computer scientist in application development.

In addition to my apprenticeship, I also work on personal projects of all types, such as BuyFair: a website with the aim of making online shopping in Switzerland more local. In my free time, I also like to write articles, manage projects or play video games.

Since November 2021, I also do freelance Web Design and Front-end Development for my clients. If you are interested, feel free to take a look at my work and services!
